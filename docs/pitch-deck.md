## Slide 4 — The Three Layers (Core IA)

# Concern → Ingredient → Product

```
LAYER 1: What problem do you have?
─────────────────────────────────────────────
  Acne  ·  Pores  ·  Hydration  ·  Brightening
  Soothing  ·  Anti-aging  ·  Sun Protection

              ↓

LAYER 2: What actually fixes that? And why?
─────────────────────────────────────────────
  Acne selected →
    BHA          "dissolves sebum inside the pore"
    Niacinamide  "controls oil, fades post-acne marks"
    Tea Tree     "antibacterial, calms inflammation"

              ↓

LAYER 3: Which products contain those ingredients?
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Shown in routine order:
  Cleanser → Toner → Serum → Moisturizer → Sunscreen
  Each card shows WHY it fits the selected concern
```

This three-layer structure is the core. Everything else builds on top of it.

## Slide 5 — Key Functions

# What Kwip does, specifically.

**Concern Selector**
Filter chips on the home screen. Select one concern. The entire page reconfigures instantly.
Single-select by design: one problem, one clear answer.

**Ingredient Highlight**
The key actives for the selected concern appear with a one-line Vietnamese explanation of what each one does. This is the "why" layer — without it, Kwip is just a filtered catalog.

**Routine-Grouped Products**
Products grouped by routine step in the order they are applied.
Answers not just "what product" but "where does it go in my routine."

**Product Detail**
Full ingredient breakdown. Purchase links. Vietnamese + English.
